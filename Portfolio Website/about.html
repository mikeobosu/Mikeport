Michael Obosu

Graphics Designer & Web Developer

I am a multidisciplinary creative and the founder of ObviousAesthetics, blending classical artistic training with data-driven technical rigor. By merging visual storytelling with analytical precision, I have led UI architecture for platforms like Ghana Transit Hub and RideMe to create designs that perfectly balance aesthetic excellence with functional performance.

My journey started in the print world, where I developed a sharp eye for typography, colour theory, and layout hierarchy. Over time, I transitioned into the digital space and fell in love with building interfaces that do not just look great — they feel intuitive and solve real problems for real people.

Today I work with startups, small businesses, and creatives across West Africa to bring their brands to life online. Whether it is a pixel-perfect Figma prototype, a fully responsive web application, or a bold brand identity, I bring the same obsessive attention to detail to every project.
